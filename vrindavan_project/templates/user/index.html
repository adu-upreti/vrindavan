About Us

Prem Pranam,

Hetauda's premier pure vegetarian restaurant. Situated conveniently on the main road, we specialize in authentic Indian vegetarian cuisine, featuring both North and South Indian delicacies. Whether you're dining in or ordering out, we guarantee the highest quality in every dish.

Don't miss our Vrindavan Misthanna Bhandar, where we serve a wide array of traditional Indian sweets. Join us for an exceptional vegetarian dining experience.

Master Chefs

Our chefs at Vrindavan bring authentic Indian flavors to every dish. Specializing in traditional cuisine and sweets, they craft meals that celebrate India's rich culinary heritage with the finest ingredients.

Quality Food

At Vrindavan, we take pride in serving high-quality, authentic vegetarian dishes. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to craft meals that honor traditional Indian flavors. Every dish is carefully prepared to ensure a delicious and wholesome dining experience.

Online Order

Enjoy the flavors of Vrindavan from the comfort of your home. We offer free delivery within a 2km range in Hetauda City. Order online and experience our delicious vegetarian dishes delivered right to your doorstep.

Chat Service

For your convenience, our chat service is available for food orders from 8 AM to 10 PM. Connect with us directly to place your order, ask questions, or get recommendations. We're here to assist you throughout the day.
