About Us

Hetauda's Home for Pure Vegetarian Food

Vrindavan Misthanna Bhandar is Hetauda's most loved 100% pure vegetarian restaurant, proudly located on Hetauda Main Road, opposite Narayani Mall. From wholesome breakfast thalis to flavourful dinners, we serve authentic Indian and Nepali vegetarian cuisine made with love — and without garlic & onion for our satvik guests.

Whether you're looking for the best biryani in Hetauda, comforting Khana Sets, crispy momos, or a warm cup of masala chai — we've got you covered, every single day from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
